Equine therapy is designed to allow the patient to connect with the horse through riding, grooming, or proximity. It has been shown to help many people with disabilities, social issues, and improve mental health.

Helps People Build:

  • Self Confidence
  • Trust
  • Patience
  • Self-Awareness
  • Social Skills
  • Problem-Solving Skills

Proven to Help People With:

  • Anxiety
  • PTSD
  • ADHD
  • Depression
  • Eating Dissorders
  • Substance Abuse

Equine therapy has proven to show high success rates within all patients who participate in some form of theraputic riding. Patiencts have an over 80% success rate of reaching therapeutic goals. Patients are also 94% more likely to seek out mental help with animal assisted therapy than one on one and there is a 0% drop out rate in many of these programs. After 3 weeks, 66.7% of patients had lower PTSD scores, and 87.5% after 6 weeks.
